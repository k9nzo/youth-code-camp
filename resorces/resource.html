Self-Care Tips

Daily Practices

  • Exercise Regularly: Physical activity can help reduce symptoms of depression.
  • Healthy Eating: A balanced diet can improve your mood and energy levels.
  • Sleep Well: Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night.

Mindfulness and Relaxation

  • Meditation: Practice mindfulness meditation to reduce stress.
  • Deep Breathing Exercises: Helps to calm the mind and body.
  • Journaling: Write down your thoughts and feelings to process emotions.

Coping Strategies

Building a Support Network

  • Talk to Friends and Family: Share your feelings with trusted individuals.
  • Join Support Groups: Connect with others who understand what you're going through.

Professional Help

  • Therapy: Seek help from a licensed therapist or counselor.
  • Medication: Consult with a healthcare provider about medication options.
